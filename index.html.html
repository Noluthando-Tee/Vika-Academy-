About Vika Academy

Our Mission

We strive and commit to deliver high quality, foster information sharing, be professional, innovative, and reliable training service to attract, develop, motivate, protect and retain a diverse workforce to address critical development issues.

Our Vission

To be the business community and learning hub for Small, Medium, and Large Enterprises.


Our Objective

• To increase quality output
• Higher return on investment (ROI) and long-term success
• Enhance quality of work and productivity
• To position and maintain our Human Capital towards the achievement o f optimal Strategy Business Objectives


We focus on practical outcomes and accredited short courses that drive immediate career growth and corporate efficiency.

Business Background

VIKA BUSINESS BACKGROUND Training Academy is a 100% Small Medium Enterprise (SME), black-owned, controlled, and managed organisation with a social purpose that brings together industry leaders and experts to develop, implement and evaluate interventions and programs to ensure maximum impact forstakeholders/recipients and return on investment. We are an equity organisation with a Level 1 B-BBEE Scorecard. We draw on global best practices to continually push your competitive edge and promote profitability and growth. We have a competitive edge over other service companies in that the turn-around time is remarkably short, while the output is incontrovertibly sublime. Our organisation focuses its services on both the public and private sectors. Our practitioners are based throughout the country and we are able to train in every area of South Africa where necessary. We believe that learning should lead to credits towards qualifications and it should enhance work performance. All our programmes are competency-based and performance- driven. We believe that a focused aligned organisation can be an unstoppable force. We are an organisation founded to empower and embolden businesses and communities to harness natural abilities for a better future society through socially responsible management and community outreach.
